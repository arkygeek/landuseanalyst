Crop Parameter Name

Enter the name of the crop parameter you are defining in this field. You do not need a unique name. You can have, for example, 10 different crop parameters defined, all named "Jericho". Landuse Analyst uses a special method of saving the crop to eliminate the issue of duplicate filenames. This way, you can have, for example, conservative and optimistic settings for each crop at a site. See Crop Parameter Notes
