Wild to Tame Ratio <<<<<<< .mine

Set the division in the diet as a ratio between calories provided by Wildsources and calories provided by Tame sources with the small sliders. You can either click and drag with the mouse, or use the mouse wheel. As you slide it, you will see that the percentages for both are automatically adjusted.

=======

The Wild to Tame Ratio is the distinction you must make when defining the settlements plant or meat portion of the diet. This setting relies on the assumption that all of their food comes from either wild sources or tame sources. To adjust, simply move the slider left or right, or spin your mouse wheel. Note that this setting is based on calories
