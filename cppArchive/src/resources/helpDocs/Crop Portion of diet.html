Crop Portion of diet

This tells LandUse Analyst how important the crop was to the populations diet. This figure is the percentage of the tame plant portion of the diet. In other words, how much this plant contiributes compared to the other crops.

A very important note is that before the model will run, the sum of this value from all the plants in the model has to equal 100%. This value is kept updated and displayed in the lower portion of the main window and is visible at all times.
