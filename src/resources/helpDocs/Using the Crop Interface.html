Using the Crop Interface

The Crop Interface has been designed to be very simple and easy to use. The top section lists all of the defined crops, and selecting one will display its settings in the two lower sections. The lower sections are divided into the crop definition details on the left, and the model parameter settings for the currently selected crop on the right.

If you wish to edit either the crop or its parameters, you simply click on the Manage button below the display areas.
