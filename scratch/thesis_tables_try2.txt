Appendix A

Botanical Data
A.1

Shuna Botanical Data

The botanical remains in the following three tables was used to calculate the approximate contributions of the major components of the diet at Shuna. All three of the
charts have been reformatted by myself, but are drawn completely from the work of
Holden which is to be included in the forthcoming monograph on Shuna by Baird and
Philip.
